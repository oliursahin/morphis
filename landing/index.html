about

morphis is a lightweight, privacy-first email client for Gmail. all your data stays on your machine. built with Rust and Tauri for a native experience with a tiny footprint.

navigation

keyboard first. navigate your entire inbox without the mouse — vim-inspired motions, command palette, instant search.

organization

automatically split incoming emails into focused tabs — Important, Calendar, GitHub, Others. fully customizable.

search

full-text search powered by Tantivy. subject, body, sender — all indexed locally.

sync

continuous background sync with Gmail. new emails appear instantly.

accounts

manage multiple accounts from a single window. each gets its own splits and search index.

privacy

everything stored in a local SQLite database. zero telemetry. open source, MIT licensed.
